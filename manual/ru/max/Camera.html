Working with Camera / 3ds Max

This page contains the information on how to setup 3ds Max's cameras to be used with Verge3D.

[contents] [anchor:setting_up_in_3ds_max]

Setting Up in 3ds Max

Simply add one of the supported cameras to you scene:

  • *Physical*
  • *Target*
  • *Free*
[anchor:camera_settings]

Camera Settings

Each camera in 3ds Max is assigned with Verge3D settings:

*Allow Panning* - allow camera panning.

*Rotate Speed* - camera rotation speed factor.

*Move Speed* - camera movevent speed factor.

*Pivot Distance Limits* - distance limits for Orbit camera.

*Vertical Angle Limits* - vertical angle limits for Orbit camera.

*Horizontal Angle Limits* - horizontal angle limits for Orbit camera.

[anchor:firstperson_settings]

First-person camera settings

*Enabled* - enable first-person camera.

*Collision Material* - collision material which will be used for the camera.

*Gaze Level* - first-person camera "gaze" (or "head"/"eyes") level.

*Story Height* - story height.

[anchor:camera_control_with_puzzles]

Camera Control with Puzzles

You can use camera puzzles to control your camera in real time.
